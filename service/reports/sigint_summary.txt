MERIDIAN DEFENSE GROUP — INTELLIGENCE REPORT
Report ID:      MDG-2024-0117
Classification: UNCLASSIFIED
Date:           17 January 2024
Author:         Signals Analysis Division

SIGINT Summary — Eastern Mediterranean

EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Routine monitoring of commercial maritime communications in the
Eastern Mediterranean basin during the period 01-15 January 2024
identified several patterns of interest.  All intercepts occurred
on unencrypted commercial frequencies and are assessed as routine
shipping coordination traffic.

KEY FINDINGS

1. Commercial vessel traffic volume increased 12% compared to the
   same period in 2023, consistent with seasonal trade patterns.

2. Three previously unlogged call signs were observed on VHF
   Channel 16.  All three were subsequently correlated with newly
   registered cargo vessels flagged in Liberia.

3. No anomalous encrypted traffic was detected on monitored
   frequency bands during this reporting period.

ASSESSMENT

The observed patterns are consistent with normal commercial maritime
operations.  No indicators of threat-related activity were identified.
Continued monitoring is recommended at the standard collection tempo.
