GEOINT Analysis — Coastal Installations

EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Multi-spectral satellite imagery analysis of coastal installations
along a 400km stretch of the Eastern Mediterranean coastline.
Imagery was collected between 15-28 March 2024 under favorable
atmospheric conditions (< 15% cloud cover on all collection days).

KEY FINDINGS

1. Three commercial port facilities show infrastructure expansion
   consistent with published development plans.  New breakwater
   construction at Site Alpha is approximately 60% complete.

2. Vegetation analysis indicates normal seasonal patterns.  No
   evidence of ground disturbance outside known construction zones.

3. Thermal analysis of industrial facilities shows output levels
   consistent with published production figures.

ASSESSMENT

No anomalous activity detected at any monitored coastal installation.
All observed changes are consistent with commercial development.
Recommend maintaining quarterly collection cycle.
