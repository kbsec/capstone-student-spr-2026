Cyber Incident Report — Perimeter Probe

EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

On 28 April 2024, network monitoring detected a series of TCP SYN
probes against the external perimeter of the corporate network.
Investigation determined this was a routine internet-wide scan
by a known security research organization.

TIMELINE

- 28 Apr 0314 UTC: First SYN probe detected (port 443)
- 28 Apr 0314-0347 UTC: Sequential port scan, ports 1-1024
- 28 Apr 0352 UTC: Scan concluded, no follow-up activity
- 28 Apr 0800 UTC: SOC analyst confirmed source as Shodan scanner

IMPACT

None.  No services were exposed beyond those already documented
in the external attack surface assessment.  All probed ports
returned expected responses (RST or no response).

ACTIONS TAKEN

- Confirmed firewall rules are current
- Added source IP range to watchlist for correlation
- No changes to security posture required
